About

Signal-first. Openly interpretive.

Sentinel is the platform layer. WW3 Barometer is the flagship public-facing product. It tracks military, financial, geopolitical, and interpretive escalation variables through a clearly labeled method.

What this is

A signal dashboard with an explicit worldview and an explicit method. It is not a neutral-by-default mainstream publication, and it does not try to flatten faith-aware or interpretive variables out of the analysis.

What this is not

This is not investment advice, not security advice, and not a browser-side AI toy. The public site loads prebuilt snapshots and exposes the categories of content clearly so readers can understand what they are reading.

Editorial stance

The platform does not claim neutrality of worldview. It does commit to clarity of method. Verified events, analytical assessments, interpretive signals, and market data are labeled separately.

Launch architecture

Day one is designed around a simple static frontend, a small Python update engine, human review before publish, archived snapshots, and last-known-good fallback behavior.
